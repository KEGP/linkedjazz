Linked Jazz project

Explore the following endpoints:

Endpoint Description Link
LODE human readable version of our ontology http://localhost:8080/ontology
WebVOWL graphical visualisation of our ontology http://localhost:8080/webvowl/
LodView human readable representation of the entities in our dataset http://localhost:8080/lodview/kejazz/[CLASSNAME][ID] (ex. http://localhost:8080/resource/kejazz/Artist/663f8232-8c46-4851-803f-a91d31593b14)
SPARQL-Anything human readable representation of the entities in our dataset http://localhost:8080/sparqlanything/sparql (remember to replace /sparql.anything with sparql.anything in the text field placed at the top of the page)
Small example application retrieve information on artists that played together in a recording and were friends http://localhost:8080/app/

The ontology source files in RDF/XML format are available, respectively, at http://localhost:8080/ontology/kejazz and at http://localhost:8080/ontology/chord-annotations.
